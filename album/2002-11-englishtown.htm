Englishtown Raceway Park
H-Body Gathering
November 10, 2002

    Well the gathering had fewer cars than expected paritally due to miscommunication resulting in some cars being turned away.   Bill Lynch organized this gathering and v8monza.com sponsored it.  

Event Winners
Best of show: Brian Fahernfeld and his blown ‘71 Vega wagon.   The graphics on this car are incredible, car was seen in the back of one of the car mags not too long ago.
Best Vega: Jim Mullen and his black blown ‘75 Vega, WOW!
Best Monza: Daryl Gustafson and his big-block ‘75 Monza coupe, the car just seems to come apart like its made with Velcro®, he had removed the whole nose to show off the big-block.
Best Cosworth: Jake Coviello and his slightly modified but original Cosworth.
Best Buick, Olds, Pontiac: Larry Heagren (Vega Larry) and his original ‘74 Astre with original plaid interior?   Checkout his homemade twin two barrel intake manifold on the 140 four cylinder engine.
Monza race winner: Bill Lynch's (Monzabill) v8monza.com sponsored ‘78 Monza beat Dryle Gustofson when Dryle red lighted.
Vega race winner and overall winner: John Loreno and his yellow and blue Vega wagon.
Winner of the balljoint adapters: Rick Rothermel from PA.

Here are a few photos taken at the event.   Please have a look and vote on your favorite by sending your e-mail vote (click on the link under the photo) and we'll give the photographer the honors of having the winning photo posted on our main page as a random image with the others.   We're looking forward to the next event soon and hopefully you'll be there.

Thanks,
Bob Gumm
