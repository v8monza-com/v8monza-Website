Island Dragway
H-Body Gathering
October 12, 2003

    Well the gathering had fewer cars than expected paritally due to weather.   Bill Lynch organized this gathering and v8monza.com sponsored it.  

Event Winners
Best of Show: Bryan McCready's black Spyder
Best Vega: Larry Heagren's (vegamanlarry) "Brown Beast"
Best Monza: Gerald Thompson's ‘79 Monza notchback.
Best Cosworth: Jake Coviello's blue Cosworth.
Best Buick, Olds, Pontiac: Ted Covalente's teal Astre.
Monza race winner: Bill Lynch's (Monzabill) v8monza.com sponsored ‘78 Monza beat Dryle Gustofson when Dryle red lighted.
Long Haul Award: Tom Lippert, Olmstead Ohio, white Cosworth.
Race Results:
12.00 and Slower: Gerald Thompson (win) and Al Schuckman (runner up)
11.99 and Faster (no electronics) John Piekart (win) and Bob Bowman (runner up)
Electronic Class: Barry Angstadt (win) and Barry Hohnarth (runner up)
Fastest Elapsed Time: Barry Hohnarth with a 9.08 ET
Best Reaction Time: Barry Hohnarth with a 501 RT
Raffles:
Balljoint Adapter Kit: Daniel figueroa '77 Mirage
Craftsman Screw Driver Set: Rob Berkowitz

Here are a few photos taken at the event. We're looking forward to the next event soon and hopefully you'll be there.
