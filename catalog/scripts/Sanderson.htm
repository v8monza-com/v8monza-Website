Sanderson CC13 Headers
P/N SAN-CC13 (P/C) (R/F/V/A)

  Available in plain painted (P) or coated (C) with ceramic silver these headers can be tailor made to fit your standard Chevy heads (R), Fastburn heads (F), Vortec heads (V), or Aftermarket heads (A).   Please specify the type of heads you intend to use.   These are shorty headers for your GM H-Body.   They'll work with just about any engine mounting system available.   This means you can use them with the factory V8 mounts, Don Hardy, Herbert-Meek/Hurst, Hooker, Motion Performance, Trans-dapt, and similar kits.   They're designed with extra-thick exhaust port flanges for the best seal we've ever seen to combat the typical annoying header leak!   They're a one-piece design which further prevents exhaust leaks that are common with multi-piece headers.   This one-piece design means that the steering column must be temporarily removed to install the left (driver's side) header since one tube goes around the steering column.   Sanderson also recommends you inspect your engine mounts, header bolts, and header to steering column clearance regularly.   This is to prevent the header from contacting the steering column which could cause steering interference during vehicle operation.   These parts are shipped directly from Sanderson.   Sometimes stock runs out because of unforeseeable events.   Because of this some items may not ship immediately and can take up to 2-4 weeks for shipping.
