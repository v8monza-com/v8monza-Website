This kit is made to fit the 1971-74 Vega only!   Includes headers with reducers, gaskets and bolts, frame adapters and transmission cross-member for 3speed, 4 speed, powerglide or 350 turbo.   Pictorial instructions also included.   Headers have 1 5/8" primaries.   Some have had success using these on 1975-80 H-Bodies too, others have reported some difficulty using the mounts on later model H-Bodies (1975 - 1980).   If installed on 1975-80 H-Bodies you'll need to fabricte weld-in spacer plates to go between the Don Hardy mount kit and the uni-body.   We don't sell the spacer plates, but they are not difficult to make.   Simply use the triangular plate on the Don Hardy mounts as a template.   Many have reported that simply installing spacer plates (like those used in factory 4-cylinder installation) between the Hardy mounts and the unibody will allow use of the Don Hardy kit in the 1975-80 H-Bodies.   If you're not converting from a 4 cylinder and don't have the 4-cyl plates (i.e. if you're converting a V6 car to a V8), you should be able make simple spacer plates out of 1/4 to 3/8 steel.   These spacer plates should be welded to the vehicle just like the factory plates are.
