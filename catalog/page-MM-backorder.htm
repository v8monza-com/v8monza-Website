These motor mounts have been specially designed and hand-made by Bob Gumm to replace your factory V8 Monza engine mounts.   Factory V8 Monza mounts are no longer available at your local parts store and even if you can find them you'll pay over $200.00 for them.   Our mounts were designed using factory mounts as a template and with economical replacement costs in mind.   After just a few months original mounts begin to sag, but not our mounts.   Our mounts are designed to use an off-the-shelf mount that by design resists sagging.   When these mounts finally do need replacement, you can pick up a replacement mount at your local parts store for about $10 or less.   Take a look at this photo of our mounts.   Remember, because these mounts maintain their original form they'll give you back the clearance between your oil pan and K-frame/steering components.   Some customers have reported reduced clearance between the engine and hood with these mounts.   Not that our mounts are taller, it's just that the original mounts sag so badly that the engine eventually sat lower.   Some folks who had the old sagging mounts had installed performance induction systems that just cleared the hood.   Our mounts bring the engine back up to where it should be and as a result there is less clearance between some induction systems and the hood.   These mounts are currently on back-order. (NOTE: Some backorders may take 6-10 weeks to ship).   Remember, these mounts will also require the factory V8 frame plates to be used on non-factory V8 installations.
