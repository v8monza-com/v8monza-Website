Don Hardy V8 Vega Kit

Includes headers with reducers, gaskets and bolts, frame adapters and transmission cross-member for 3speed, 4 speed, powerglide or 350 turbo. Pictorial instructions also included. Headers have 1 5/8" primaries. Some have had success using these on the Chevy Monza too, others have reported some difficulty using the mounts on later model H-Bodies (1975 - 1980). I have not tried the Don Hardy mounts, but it would seem to me that it would not be too difficult to get them to work. Many have reported that simply installing spacer plates (like those used in factory 4-cylinder installation) between the Hardy mounts and the unibody will allow use of the Don Hardy kit in the 1975-80 H-Bodies. If you don't have the 4-cyl plates, you should be able make simple spacer plates out of 1/4 to 3/8 steel. NOTE: Don Hardy charges a 25% restocking fee.
