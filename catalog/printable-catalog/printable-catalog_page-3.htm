Available in Plain Steel (P), Silver Ceramic Coated (SEC), or now Black Ceramic Coated (BEC) these Block Hugger headers are made to fit your standard Small Block Chevy heads whether they are regular factory style heads, Angle plug style heads (AP) or "D" Port style heads (DP). These are shorty headers for your GM H-Body. They'll work with just about any engine mounting system available. This means you can use them with the factory V8 mounts, Don Hardy, Herbert-Meek/Hurst, Hooker, Motion Performance, Trans-dapt, and similar kits. They're designed with extra-thick exhaust port flanges for the best seal we've ever seen to combat the typical annoying header leak!. They're a one-piece design which further prevents exhaust leaks that are common with multi-piece headers. This one-piece design means that the steering column must be temporarily removed to install the left (driver's side) header since one tube goes around the steering column. Sanderson also recommends you inspect your engine mounts, header bolts, and header to steering column clearance regularly to prevent the header from contacting the steering column causing steering interference.
