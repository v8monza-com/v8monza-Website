I keep getting questions from people who want to know what springs thay should install for their particular vehicle.   Problem is, there are always variables.   Such as, 1971-1974 Vegas had shallow pockets in the front lower control arms.   Swapping in a set of factory V8 Monza springs without changing the lower control arms will result in a nose up attitude.   I hope this chart will help you find what your looking for.   Print this list and use it when you're buying springs and looking for complementary components.  

Happy Hunting,
