Instrument Panel

Monzas came with two types of instrument panels. You could get full instrumentation or idiot lights. I have colorized these diagrams for ease of interpretation. Of course, the printed circuits behind your instrument panels will not be colored. (They are copper laminated on a plastic sheet.)
