Dashboard Wiring

This is the main wiring harness behind the dashboard. It hooks up to the harnesses illustrated in the Passenger Compartment and Trunk Wiring Diagrams and Engine Compartment Wiring Diagram. I did some minor revisions on 01 April 1998 just to make it easier to read. As always, the circuitry is box stock--just as it came from the General.

The effort involved in creating this diagram was incredible. Many thanks to the General for the original diagram, Bob Gumm for providing the intial impetus, Joe Cook for his unending moral support, and Jasc, Inc. for their Paint Shop Pro 4 shareware without which this (and all the others) would not have been possible.
