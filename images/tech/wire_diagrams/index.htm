Wiring Diagrams

All information contained herein is provided with the understanding that it will be used by you at your own risk and that I, Mike Gomez, will bear no responsibility if you smoke your car (or worse.) If that sounds slightly unfriendly, well...you probably won't be in a really good mood the first time you make that funny smelling smoke.

The basic source of these diagrams is Chevrolet document No. ST 359-77. These are wiring diagrams for 1977 model cars. However, they should be generally applicable to Monzas of all years.

They have been colorized, simplified, rearranged, and otherwise edited for ease of use. In every case, the original circuitry is followed. If you feel like experimenting, go ahead at your own risk. I feel it is best to stick with the original design as long as it performs the desired function. All electrical work should be performed by a competent electrician who may or may not be yourself!

Note: I found minor errors in the original diagrams which I have corrected. There may be others I haven't spotted yet. If anyone sees anything dubious, please send e-mail so we can hash it out.

Have fun! Good luck!
