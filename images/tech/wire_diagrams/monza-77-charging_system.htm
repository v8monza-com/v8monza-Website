Charging System

Here's the charging system. Pretty simple, huh?

Those fusible links (2E & 2F) are important. Accept no substitutes. If you doubt the authenticity of your fusible links, go to your friendly auto parts supplier and get some.

Don't worry about the GND terminal on the alternator not having anything connected to it. The alternator is well-grounded just by mounting it on the engine. If you really want to be anal about it, go ahead and run a wire from there to the block or to the chassis. The gauge doesn't matter (since the darned thing is ALREADY grounded.) Take my word for it.

The little brown wire runs the alternator idiot light. It also creates trouble occasionally by feeding-back power to the ignition system after the switch has been selected to the "OFF" position. This causes the engine to run until something is disconnected under the hood.

This problem will be tackled in the "Custom Wiring" section (as soon as I get around to writing it)along with suggestions for neat stuff to do with that 2H feed if you're not air conditioned.
