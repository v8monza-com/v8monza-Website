Trunk Wiring

The circuitry of both the coupes and fastbacks are essentially identical. The only real difference is that the coupes have separate directional signal lights while the fastbacks combine the directional signals with the stop and tail lights.

The harness that goes through the passenger compartment is the same for both except the coupe harness has an extra 18 gauge red wire for the brake lights. The fastback harness may incorporate this wire. However, it is not used in these cars. You may tap this wire for a center high-mounted stop light (CHMSL) if desired.
